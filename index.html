If you're one of the millions of people who are reclaiming the backyard as a productive space - whether it's as a hobby or a pursuit of a fully self-sufficient way of life - the Grow Your Own Tracker can help you grow better. Easily track what you grow using email or beautiful web and mobile apps - then compare it to friends and neighbours to see how you stack up. Get points for uploading photographs and recording how much your produce, then track this production over time to identify trends and see where you could be doing better. Get motivated by exchanging your points for free seeds and money off coupons.

It's not just for tracking though. We'll help you do grow better by suggesting new things to grow and additional steps you could take to becoming more sustainable - complete these suggestions, and get points! If you're not into the whole gamification and social aspect, you can completely disable it all and still make full use of our great harvest tracker and pretty graphs.

FAQ

Why are you doing this?
I've been trying out various ways to track what I grow and none of them are good enough. I want something simple, yet with enough innovative features that make it more fun to use than just a spreadsheet. I want to know if everyone else around me is doing badly with tomatoes this year or if it's just me - and what I can do to improve.
Free seeds?
That's the plan. If you participate in the social and gamification aspects of the site, you'll get points for taking simple actions like recording your harvest everyday and uploading a picture, or showing off your bounty to friends. You can then exchange those points for free seeds or other promotional items. We want to reward you for doing your bit to help make our world that little bit more sustainable.
